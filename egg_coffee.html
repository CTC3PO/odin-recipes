Vietnamese Egg Coffee

Whipping an egg yolk into sweetened condensed milk will give you a meringue-like, silky, smooth fluff. Top your coffee or iced coffee with this topping and you will have a Vietnamese specialty coffee.

Information

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 5 mins
  • Total: 1 mins
  • Yield: 1 coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup hot strong coffee

Directions

  1. Step 1: Place egg yolk and condensed milk in a tall jar. Whip with a milk frother until soft peaks form.
  2. Step 2: Pour hot coffee into a mug and top with frothed milk.
